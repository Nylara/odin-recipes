Sweet and Salty Holiday Toffee

by Glen and Friends

This is a great recipe!

Ingredients
  • 1 sleeve graham crackers(or saltines)
  • 250 ml of water
  • 250 ml packed brown sugar
  • 500 ml semisweet chocolate chips
  • handful of Smarties or M&Ms
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 180ºC (350°F).
  2. Line a 9x13 baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Cover the baking sheet with graham crackers.
  4. In saucepan, melt together butter and brown sugar.
  5. Bring to a boil, cook stirring continuously for about 2 minutes.
  6. Pour mixture over crackers in an even layer; bake for about 15 minutes.
  7. Immediately after pulling from oven top with chocolate chips and let melt.
  8. Smooth the chocolate into an even layer, and then sprinkle with Smarties.
  9. Cut into squares, then cool until the chocolate has hardened.
